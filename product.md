# Vetta.ai Product Overview

## Core Product
Vetta.ai is an AI-powered interview preparation platform that helps job seekers practice realistic interviews, improve communication, and receive structured feedback they can act on quickly.

The product simulates interview scenarios (role-specific and skill-specific), captures candidate responses, and turns each practice session into clear coaching insights such as:
- what went well,
- what needs improvement,
- and what to practice next.

## Core Use Cases
- Practice mock interviews before real hiring rounds.
- Improve answer quality for behavioral and technical questions.
- Build confidence for live interviews through repeated guided practice.
- Track progress over time across sessions, roles, and question categories.
- Prepare targeted interview responses based on resume and job goals.

## Target Audience
- Students and fresh graduates preparing for internships and entry-level roles.
- Early-career and mid-career professionals switching jobs or domains.
- Candidates returning to the job market after a break.
- Non-native English speakers who want to improve interview communication.
- Career coaches or training programs that want scalable interview practice support.

## User Problems We Solve
- “I don’t know how to practice interviews effectively on my own.”
- “I’m not getting enough specific feedback after mock practice.”
- “I struggle to structure my answers clearly under pressure.”
- “I feel underconfident even when I know the content.”
- “I can’t tell whether I’m actually improving over time.”

## Product Value Proposition
Vetta.ai gives users a fast, repeatable way to convert interview practice into measurable improvement. Instead of generic tips, users get role-relevant simulations and actionable feedback they can immediately apply to the next interview.

## Primary Outcomes
- Better interview readiness and confidence.
- Higher quality, more structured responses.
- Reduced preparation time through focused practice.
- Stronger conversion from interviews to offers.

## Scope (Current Product Definition)
At its core, Vetta.ai focuses on:
- interview simulation,
- response analysis,
- personalized feedback,
- and progress tracking.
